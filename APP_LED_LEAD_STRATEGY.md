# App-Led Lead Strategy

## Goal

Add a second lead-generation layer on top of the migration signals:

- migration / change triggers
- app and stack maturity signals

This gives you a way to target companies that are already revenue-generating and operationally serious because they use commercial tooling.

## Why This Matters

If a domain is using paid or business-critical tools, that usually suggests:

- active online revenue generation
- some internal marketing maturity
- a real operating business rather than a dormant site
- better odds they understand software, growth, and technical work

That makes these leads more commercially attractive, even when they are not perfect migration matches.

## Best App-Led Lead Buckets

### 1. Revenue-Generating Commerce Stacks

Use:

- `eCommerce Platform`
- `Payment Platforms`

Examples:

- Shopify + multiple payment methods
- Shopify Plus + Shopify Pay + PayPal
- BigCommerce + Klarna + PayPal
- Magento + Braintree / CardinalCommerce
- WooCommerce + payment plugins

Why it matters:

- these sites are clearly transacting
- payment complexity often creates SEO and UX friction during migrations
- payment-heavy stacks often indicate meaningful commercial intent

## 2. SEO-Mature Or SEO-Adjacent CMS Stacks

Use:

- `CMS Platform`

Examples from current data:

- WordPress
- Duda
- Unbounce

Why it matters:

- WordPress often means content-led growth is possible
- Unbounce suggests landing-page and acquisition activity
- mixed CMS plus commerce stacks can indicate technical complexity

## 3. CRM-Backed Revenue Teams

Use:

- `CRM Platform`

Examples from current data:

- Salesforce
- retail CRM

Why it matters:

- CRM usage suggests a structured sales or lifecycle process
- these companies are more likely to care about lead attribution and funnel quality
- SEO can be framed as pipeline contribution, not just rankings

## 4. Marketing Automation Users

Use:

- `Marketing Automation Platform`

Examples from current data:

- Klaviyo
- Active Campaign
- Curalate
- Rebuy
- Care Cart
- Zoho PageSense

Why it matters:

- these brands are already investing in acquisition and retention
- they are often easier to sell to than businesses with no martech layer
- they usually have more measurable revenue levers

## 5. Infrastructure-Maturity Leads

Use:

- `Hosting Provider`

Examples from current data:

- Shopify Hosted
- Cloudflare Hosting
- Kinsta
- Amazon
- Pantheon

Why it matters:

- a stronger hosting stack often means larger or more serious operations
- infrastructure changes often overlap with SEO performance, crawlability, and migrations

## Best App-Led Prospecting Angles

The strongest additional lead sets to create are:

### Shopify Or Shopify Plus + Klaviyo

Why:

- usually a serious ecommerce operator
- already spending on retention and email
- likely to value category, collection, and lifecycle SEO

### Magento Or BigCommerce + Salesforce / CRM

Why:

- stronger sales process
- likely larger average deal size
- easier to position SEO as pipeline contribution

### WordPress + WooCommerce + Cloudflare / Kinsta

Why:

- active content + commerce hybrid
- technical SEO and performance hooks are easy to frame

### Any Commerce Platform + Multiple Payment Tools

Why:

- strong proof of monetisation
- usually a real store, not a placeholder
- better commercial-fit filter

### Any Stack + Unbounce

Why:

- indicates active paid acquisition or lead gen
- often means landing-page sprawl and indexation opportunities

## How To Add This To Lead Scoring

Add an `app_maturity_score` or `revenue_stack_score` layer.

Suggested logic:

- +3 if commercial ecommerce platform is present
- +2 if multiple payment platforms are present
- +2 if CRM platform is present
- +2 if marketing automation platform is present
- +1 if premium hosting / infra signal is present
- +1 if content-growth CMS like WordPress / Unbounce is present

This should not replace migration scoring.
It should improve prioritisation within the migration and outreach lists.

## Practical Rule

Use app-led buckets as a quality multiplier, not as the only signal.

Best formula:

- trigger signal
- plus stack maturity
- plus contactability

That combination gives you the best prospecting shortlist.
